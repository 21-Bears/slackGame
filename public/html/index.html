Round Table Battle

Need a diversion from the normal slack conversation?
Want to get your competitive juices flowing?

Sounds like you need to battle.

Play an exciting turn-based strategy against another slack team member.

Start Command

/rtb start or join a game

Movement Commands

Dash Appears on the attack menu. You are able to move two spaces clock wise or counter clockwise but you are then unable to attack.

Counter-clockwise X 2 Move counter clockwise two spaces but unable to attack

Counter-clockwise Move counter clockwise one space then attack

Stay Maintain current position and attack

Clockwise Move clockwise one space and attack

Clockwise X 2 Move clockwise two spaces but unable to attack

Attack Commands

attackA attack tile directly across from you

attackB attack the tile five spaces left and right from your location

attackC attack the tile four spaces left and right from your location

attackD attack the tile directly on your left and right

Information Commands

/rtb help Get a brief overview of the game

/rtb topTen Get a list of the top ten best players

/rtb myRank See where you rank against the competition
