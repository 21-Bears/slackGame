SlackGame Bot

Need a diversion from the normal slack conversation?
Want to get your competitive juices flowing?

With SlackGame bot you can.

Play an exciting turn-based strategy against another slack team member.

Basic Commands

/COMMAND play start or join a game

/COMMAND help get some help

Movement Commands

/COMMAND move-left move one space to the left

/COMMAND move-left-2 move two spaces to the left

/COMMAND move-right move one space to the right

/COMMAND move-right-2 move two spaces to the right

Attack Commands

/COMMAND attackA attack tile directly across from you

/COMMAND attackB

attack the tile five spaces left and right from your location

/COMMAND attackC attack the tile four spaces left and right from your location

/COMMAND attackD attack the tile directly on your left and right
